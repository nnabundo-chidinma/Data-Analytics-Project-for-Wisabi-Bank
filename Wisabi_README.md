**Wisabi Bank ATM Transactions Analysis**

**Problem**
Wisabi Bank needed to understand ATM usage patterns across 5 locations in Nigeria.

**Tools Used**
Excel (Power Pivot, Pivot Table

**Key Questions Answered**
- Which ATM location has the highest transactions? Kano
- Which age group transacts the most? 25-34
- What are the peak transaction times? 4 pm - 5 pm

**Key Findings**
1. Deposits lag behind Withdrawals and Transfers across all locations, and this shows a gap in
   deposit convenience. Introducing POS terminals could reduce ATM congestion and make depositing
   more accessible for customers who find ATM deposits stressful
2. Since ages 25-34 are the age group that performs the highest transactions, to maximise ATM utilisation,
   Wisabi Bank should prioritise ATM placement in locations this demographic frequently visits, including
   but not limited to bus stops, bars, markets, and places of worship, rather than concentrating solely on
   traditional banking areas
